Vegan Chocolate Cake

Description

If you're out of eggs or butter but you're craving cake, this is the perfect recipe. Super easy!

Ingredients

  • 200g plain flour
  • 200g granulated sugar
  • 75g cocoa powder
  • 16g baking powder
  • 450ml milk of your choice
  • 35ml vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 160 Celsius. Grease a cake or loaf tin
  2. Sift the flour and cocoa powder into a large bowl. Add the sugar and baking powder and combine with a whisk
  3. In another bowl, combine the wet ingredients
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry, and mix until just combined. Don't over-mix or your cake will be very heavy.
  5. Pour the batter into the cake tin and bake for around 40 minutes, or until baked through
  6. Turn out onto a wire rack to cool
  7. Serve with ice cream or fruit
