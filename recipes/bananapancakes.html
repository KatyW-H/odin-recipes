Banana Pancakes

Description

These quick and easy banana pancakes are perfect for those following a vegan diet, or for when you're out of eggs. Feel free to adapt them to your tastes by adding chocolate chips, fresh or dried fruit, cinammon or any other flavourings you like!

Makes 4-6 pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 overripe banana
  • 300ml soy milk (can be substituted for any dairy or non-dairy milk)
  • 1 cup plain flour (substitute oat flour if following a gluten-free diet)
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • A splash of vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mash the banana into a puree using a fork
  2. Add the milk and stir to combine thoroughly
  3. In another bowl, sift together the flour and baking powder
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet, stirring continuously with a whisk or fork to avoid lumps
  5. In a non-stick frying pan, heat the oil on a medium high heat
  6. Add dollops of the batter to the heated pan
  7. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side (note that the second side will cook much faster than the first)
  8. Serve and top with your favourite topping, syrup or sauce
