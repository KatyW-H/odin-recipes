Ratatouille

Description

A hearty yet sweet vegetable stew which can be either enjoyed as a side dish or served with bread, rice or potatoes as a main.

Serves 3-4

Ingredients

  • 1 aubergine
  • 1 courgette
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 yellow pepper
  • 2-3 fresh tomatoes
  • 1 400g can of plum tomatoes
  • 1 sprig basil
  • 1 sprig thyme
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Chop the aubergine, courgette and peppers into 2cm pieces, cut the onion into wedges and roughly chop the fresh tomatoes
  2. Heat the oil in a large pan or pot, add the aubergine, courgette and peppers and cook until softened and golden but not fully cooked. Remove them from the pan and set aside
  3. Add the onion, basil and thyme leaves to the pan, and cook until the onion is softened
  4. Return the other vegetables to the pan, along with the tinned and fresh tomatoes and the balsamic vinegar. Stir well to combine everything, adding salt and pepper to taste
  5. Cover the pan and simmer on a low heat for 30-35 minutes until reduced
  6. Serve with some fresh basil leaves to garnish
